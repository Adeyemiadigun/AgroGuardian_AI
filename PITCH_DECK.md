## Slide 2: The Problem (The Risk & Data Trap)
Smallholder farmers are trapped in a high-risk, low-data cycle:
*   **Yield Loss:** 40% of production is lost annually to preventable diseases and climate shocks.
*   **Financial Exclusion:** Deemed "unbankable" by lenders due to a lack of verifiable risk data.
*   **Market Exclusion:** Locked out of the **$2B carbon market** because manual MRV (Measurement, Reporting, and Verification) audits cost **$5,000+** per farm.

## Slide 4: The Daily Hook (Yield Protection & Farm OS)
**Expert diagnostics and management in the farmer's pocket.**
*   **Multi-Modal AI Vision:** Farmers upload photos of sick livestock or crops. Our species-aware AI Vet routes the prompt to provide accurate treatments and prevention plans instantly.
*   **Automated Farm OS:** Digitizes the farm lifecycle with precision breeding tracking, feeding schedules, and inventory management.
*   *Why it matters:* This immediate, day-to-day value guarantees high user retention and continuous data generation.

## Slide 5: The Financial Unlock (Carbon MRV & Resilience)
**Turning daily activities into bankable data.**
*   **Autonomous MRV:** Validates regenerative practices (No-till, Mulching) via "Before/After" AI vision, secured by GPS/Timestamp geofencing. Cuts audit costs by over 90%.
*   **The Resilience Score (0-100):** Our engine dynamically calculates a risk profile based on crop diversity, resolved diagnoses, and climate exposure.
*   **Climate Oracle:** 69% predictive accuracy using a proprietary engine that adjusts flood/drought risk based on specific **soil types** and irrigation methods.

## Slide 6: Under the Hood (The Hackathon Flex)
**Built for scale, reliability, and low-bandwidth rural environments.**
*   **Multi-LLM Architecture:** Leverages Gemini (vision/context) and OpenAI fallbacks for highly specific, structured JSON diagnostic outputs.
*   **Asynchronous Queuing:** Uses Redis & BullMQ. Diagnostic jobs, weather syncs, and resilience scoring run in the background, ensuring the app never hangs in poor network conditions.
*   **Stack:** Node.js, Express, React, MongoDB, Cloudinary.
